About Sam

“Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once he grows up.” ― Pablo Picasso

I remember lazy childhood summer afternoons, sitting in the garden with my pencil and sketch book observing the flower and plants. Maybe this is why I love art so much because it takes me back to these childhood memories.

I specialise in pencil drawings but also do a lot of work using acrylic paints.

I also make my own paper by recycling unwanted paper found at home and in the office and turn it into something beautiful.
